BROUGHT ON TO DESIGN THEIR SOCIAL MEDIA CONTENT IN A CONSISTENT

ON PLAYER PROFILES AND LEAGUE ANNOUNCEMENTS, GIVING THE LEAGUE A FRESH
IDENTITY AND COLLECTIBLE FEEL. THE AIM WAS TO MAKE THEIR POSTS STAND OUT
WHILE KEEPING THE BRANDING RECOGNIZABLE AND ENGAGING ACROSS PLATFORMS.
