A COLLEGE PROJECT FOCUSED ON REBRANDING SULA VINEYARDS’
SHIRAZ CABERNET USING VISUAL CUES FROM BAROQUE ART. THE
GOAL WAS TO ELEVATE THE WINE’S PERSONALITY INTO A MORE PREMIUM
AND COLLECTIBLE DIRECTION WHILE RETAINING ITS IDENTITY.
