THIS BOOKLET WAS DESIGNED AS A COLLEGE PROJECT TO EXPLORE HELVETICA
BEYOND A TYPEFACE. IT COVERS ITS ORIGINS, EVOLUTION AND CULTURAL IMPACT,
WHILE ALSO DEMONSTRATING HOW HELVETICA CAN FUNCTION AS A VISUAL ELEMENT
IN DESIGN AND NOT ONLY TEXT. INSTEAD OF USING THE TRADITIONAL SWISS RED,
THE COLOUR PALETTE WAS SHIFTED TO ORANGE TO GIVE THE BOOKLET A FRESH IDENTITY
AND HELP IT STAND OUT WHILE STILL MAINTAINING A MODERNIST STRUCTURE.
